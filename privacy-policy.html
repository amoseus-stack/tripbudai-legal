Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2025

TripBudAI ("we", "our", or "us") operates the TripBudAI mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address and name when you register
  • Financial data: expense amounts, categories, currencies, and trip budgets you enter
  • Voice data: audio recordings when using voice expense entry (processed and immediately discarded)
  • Receipt images: photos you upload for receipt scanning (processed and immediately discarded)
  • Usage data: app interactions and crash reports

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and maintain the TripBudAI service
  • To process voice recordings and receipt images via OpenAI APIs
  • To sync your data across devices via Supabase
  • To send trip member invitations at your request
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • OpenAI – AI features including voice transcription and receipt scanning (openai.com)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers. We implement industry-standard security measures. Voice and image data is not stored after processing.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

TripBudAI is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
